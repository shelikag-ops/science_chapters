☀️Sun heats water
→
⬆️Water rises (vapor)

☁️Forms clouds

🌧Falls as rain

🌊Back to water!

🔥 Evaporation: Water → Vapor

💧 Liquid water
In oceans, lakes, puddles, your soup.
☀️ Sun adds heat
Particles speed UP.
💨 Vapor rises
Particles fly up into the air as a GAS!
🫥 Invisible
You can't see vapor — but it's there.

Tap each box!

❄️ Condensation: Vapor → Drops

💨 Vapor rises
Goes high up where it's COLDER.
🥶 Cools down
Particles slow, clump together.
💧 Forms drops
These tiny drops = a CLOUD!
🪟 Also at home
On a cold glass: drops appear (vapor condensing).

Tap each box!

🌧 Demo: Water Cycle in a Jar

Put hot water in a jar. Cover with a plate of ICE. WATCH!

  • Hot water = evaporation (vapor rises)
  • Cold plate = condensation (drops form)
  • Drops fall back as "rain"!
💡 You just made a mini water cycle!

🕵️ MYSTERY #1

Why does a glass SWEAT on a hot day?

Pour cold lemonade into a glass. Drops form on the OUTSIDE. Did the glass leak?

No leak! Air around the glass has water VAPOR in it. The cold glass cools the air, vapor CONDENSES into drops on the outside!

🕵️ MYSTERY #2

Where does laundry water GO?

Hang wet clothes outside. Hours later: dry. Where did the water go?

It EVAPORATED! Sun and warm air gave water particles enough energy to fly off as vapor — into the atmosphere.

🕵️ MYSTERY #3

Where did dinosaur water GO?

Dinosaurs drank water 65 million years ago. Where is that water now?

Right HERE! Water is recycled forever. The water in your cup may have been drunk by a T-Rex! Pretty wild!

🌍 Why The Water Cycle Matters

🌧 Brings rain
Plants and animals need it!
🚰 Cleans water
Evaporation leaves dirt behind!
🌡 Cools Earth
Evaporation removes heat.
💧 Recycles forever
No "new" water — same water, again and again!

✨ What you now know

  • Water cycle = sun → evaporation → clouds → rain → repeat
  • Evaporation: liquid → gas (vapor rises)
  • Condensation: gas → liquid (drops form)
  • Precipitation: rain, snow, sleet, hail
  • Same water has cycled for billions of years!
