🔭 Today's question

Is air really REAL?

Air is invisible. You can't taste, see, or hold it. But is it actually THERE? Or is it just empty space?

🤔 Today's mission: prove that air is a real substance — it takes up space, has weight, and pushes on things.
💨→🌬

💨 Air: What We Know

👁 Invisible
You can't see it normally.
🌬 Movable
You can FEEL it on a windy day.
🎈 Fillable
It can fill balloons (so it takes up space).
🪶 Lifts things
Helps balloons float, kites fly, fans cool you.

Tap each box!

🧪 Demo: Cup-in-Water Trick

Try this: stuff a paper towel in the bottom of a cup. Push the cup UPSIDE-DOWN into water.

  • Pull it out — paper towel is DRY!
  • Why? AIR was inside the cup. It blocked water from getting in.
  • Air takes up SPACE — it pushes water away.
💡 Air is a real substance — it occupies space.

⚖️ Demo: Air Has WEIGHT

Get 2 identical balloons. Tie them on a stick like a balance.

  • Pop one balloon (let air out).
  • The empty side rises! The full side sinks down.
  • Why? The full balloon has more AIR — and air HAS weight.
💡 Air is heavier than nothing — every breath weighs something!

🕵️ MYSTERY #1

Why does a parachute slow you down?

Jump from a chair without one — fast fall. With a parachute — slow fall. Why?

Air pushes UP against the big parachute! More surface = more air pushing back. Air is a substance, and it RESISTS movement.

🕵️ MYSTERY #2

Why does a straw work?

Suck on a straw — drink rises up. But you didn't pull the drink. What did?

AIR PRESSURE! When you suck, you remove air from the straw. Outside air pushes the drink UP into the straw. Air pressure does the work!

🕵️ MYSTERY #3

Why can't you breathe in space?

Astronauts need spacesuits. Why can't they just breathe normally?

There's no AIR in space! No atmosphere. Earth is special — it has a layer of air around it that lets us breathe and protects us.

🔬 Air Has Many Properties

Air can DO lots of things, just like water or rock can.

  • Air pushes on things (pressure)
  • Air can be COMPRESSED (squeezed into smaller space) — like in a tire
  • Air can be HEATED → expands and rises (hot-air balloons!)
  • Air carries sound, smells, and water vapor

✨ What you now know

  • Air IS a real substance — not "nothing"
  • Air takes up SPACE (cup demo)
  • Air has WEIGHT (balloon demo)
  • Air pushes (parachutes, straws, balloons)
  • Without air, there'd be no breathing, no wind, no weather!
