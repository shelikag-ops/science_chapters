## Decisions made

- **US bees primary, India optional** — focus is NSB Elementary (3rd Grade division) + NSF JSC. Optional practice: SOF ISO Class 3 (Nov/Dec 2026) + NSTSE Class 3 (Jan 2027).
- **Spectrum Science Grade 3 + DK First Science Encyclopedia + word jar** as the Phase A book stack (~$28).
- **Grade 3 everywhere** — Sarah's competition-window grade. Earlier Grade-2 framing was wrong-headed conservatism.
- **Reuse the existing worker** — no new infrastructure for Notion sync. The bee app reads `hs-notion-key` from the same localStorage the dashboard uses.
- **Bee quiz framework v3** — 5 categories × 3 questions + 5 cross-cutting rules. Mirrored on both the generator (Claude) and reviewer (Gemini) sides.
- **Hybrid reading strategy** — chapter-matched DK during Phase A, sweep-the-rest in Phase B, drill-weak-spots in Phase C+.
- **NASA fills the astronomy gap** — Space Place articles substitute for DK on Phase 6 chapters (d2, d3, d3a in particular).
