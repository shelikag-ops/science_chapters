# 2027 Science Bee Prep — Sarah

The living plan for Sarah's 2026-27 science bee/olympiad season. She is currently in 2nd grade and will compete as a 2nd/3rd grader through 2027. **Focus is US bees only**; India events are listed as **purely optional** practice exams.

Bee prep runs in parallel with her BFSU chapter work — bee prep is *augmentation*, not a separate track.

---

## Primary targets (US)

| # | Bee | Format | Window | Eligibility |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **National Science Bee Elementary (IAC) — 2nd Grade & Younger or 3rd Grade Division** | Online qualifier (50 MCQ / 20 min) → regional buzzer rounds → Nationals | Qualifiers Sep 2026-Mar 2027 (up to 4 attempts); Nationals **May 21-25, 2027** (Orlando) | Open; new "2nd Grade & Younger" division at Nationals |
| 2 | **NorthSouth Foundation Junior Science Bee (JSC)** | Online, 30 Q / 30 min | Regional Apr-Jun **2027**; Nationals Aug 2027 | Grades 1-3 |

That's it for required commitments. Two US bees, both online-friendly, both cleanly age-matched.

## Optional India events (Oct 2026 - Feb 2027 window)

These are **practice exams**, not competitions to chase. Skip any of them if Sarah is stressed or chapter pace is slipping. They serve one purpose: written-MCQ format practice that helps her feel comfortable with timed exams before the NSB qualifier matters.

| Event | Date | Format | Why bother |
|---|---|---|---|
| **SOF International Science Olympiad (ISO) — Class 2** | Nov 19 or Dec 3, 2026 | 35 MCQ / 60 min | Cleanest written-MCQ practice; format mimics NSB qualifier closely. Pick ONE date, not both. |
| **NSTSE Class 2 (Unified Council)** | Jan 2027 | 90 min, MCQ, no negative marking | NCERT-style phrasing is different from NSB; useful diversification. Single attempt. |

Cut from earlier plan: Silverzone iOS (heavily redundant with SOF), SOF Class 3 (too much stretch given chapter throughput).

## 12-month phased plan

### Phase A — Chapter sprint + foundation (May-Sep 2026, ~5 months)
**Primary:** finishing 36 BFSU chapters per [`../tools/chapter-session-tracker.md`](../tools/chapter-session-tracker.md). Projected finish **Oct 9, 2026**.
**Bee prep:** daily 15-min DK + workbook routine per [`../tools/dk-workbook-routine.md`](../tools/dk-workbook-routine.md). No timed practice yet, no mocks.

**Milestones:**
- End of June: ~25 cards in word jar; she's read DK Living Things + Forces sections
- End of August: ~40 cards; ~30 chapters done; comfortable doing 1 workbook page in 10 min
- Mid-September: register for NSB Elementary online qualifier (window opens)

### Phase B — First NSB qualifier + optional India warm-up (Oct-Dec 2026, ~3 months)
**Chapters:** done by early Oct. Time freed up for:
- Spiral review of weak topics (use word jar misses as the index)
- Bee mode runs of completed chapter quizzes (per [`../tools/bee-mode-spec.md`](../tools/bee-mode-spec.md))

**Bee prep:**
- Late Sep / early Oct: **first NSB qualifier attempt** — pure baseline, no pressure, results tell us where her gaps are
- Drop Spectrum workbook → switch to actual NSB practice questions from the [IAC past questions archive](https://www.iacompetitions.com/national-science-bee-middle-school-and-elementary-past-questions/)
- Continue DK reading + word jar daily
- **Optional:** SOF ISO Class 2 (Nov 19 OR Dec 3) — only if Phase A finished cleanly

**Milestones:**
- NSB qualifier 1 score: target 30/50+ as a baseline (median is around 25-30 for first attempts)
- Identify 3 weak topic areas; add to chapter retrofit / DK re-read priority

### Phase C — NSB qualifier attempts 2-3 + optional NSTSE (Jan-Feb 2027, ~2 months)
**Bee prep:**
- Daily 30 min: alternate written practice (Mon/Wed/Fri) and oral buzzer practice (Tue/Thu) — parent reads question aloud, she answers within 5 sec
- Weekly: 1 mock NSB qualifier (50 MCQ / 20 min from past questions)
- **Optional:** NSTSE Class 2 in Jan
- **NSB qualifier attempt 2** in Jan (target 35/50+)
- **NSB qualifier attempt 3** in Feb (target 40/50+)

**Milestone:** if she scores in the qualifying range on attempt 2 or 3, she's invited to a Regional Tournament. Otherwise plan for attempt 4.

### Phase D — NSB qualifier attempt 4 + Regional + NSF JSC (Mar-Jun 2027, ~4 months)
**Bee prep:**
- Final NSB qualifier attempt in early March if needed
- If she qualified: prep for Regional Tournament — buzzer drills increase to 2 per week, full mock tournaments on Saturdays
- April-June: NSF JSC regional (treat as the easy bee, victory lap)
- Final 2 weeks before any tournament: light review only, no new material, full sleep

### Phase E — Capstone (May-Aug 2027)
- **NSB Nationals** May 21-25, 2027 in Orlando if she qualifies through Regionals
- **NSF JSC Nationals** Aug 2027 if she places in the JSC regional

## Resource stack — US focus

### Books (own these now, ~$28)
- **DK First Encyclopedia of Science** (ages 6-9, ~$13) — visual recall, the spine of fact-density training. [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=DK+First+Encyclopedia+of+Science)
- **Spectrum Science Grade 3** (~$10-12) — US-curriculum aligned reading + comprehension. **Note: Spectrum Science series starts at Grade 3 (no Grade 2 edition exists).** Sarah will be in 3rd grade during the entire competition window, so Grade 3 is the right level from day one. [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Workbooks-Activities-Classroom-Homeschool-Curriculum/dp/1483811670)
- Index cards + jar for vocab tracking (~$3)

- **180 Days of Science Grade 3** (Shell Education, ~$15) — daily 1-page systematic drill

## How prep slots into the chapter app

The BFSU chapters (per [`../../CHAPTER_QUALITY_GUIDE.md`](../../CHAPTER_QUALITY_GUIDE.md)) already include 30-question quizzes with explicit "SciOly Tier-3 trap" misconception callouts (see `chapter-d3a-directions.html` slide 4a). Bee prep adds a **mode toggle**:

- **Mastery mode** (current default): hints enabled, CER homework, untimed
- **Bee mode** (new): hints stripped, 30-second per-question timer, shuffled order, MCQ-only, score+speed tracked

Implementation spec lives at [`../tools/bee-mode-spec.md`](../tools/bee-mode-spec.md). Per-chapter bee-topic mapping at [`chapter-bee-topic-map.md`](chapter-bee-topic-map.md). Bee mode goes live in Phase B (Oct 2026 onward), once enough chapters are complete to make the toggle useful.
