# Chapter → DK / Spectrum / NASA Mapping

For each of the 36 BFSU chapters Sarah works through, what DK First Science Encyclopedia section to read alongside it, what Spectrum Science Grade 3 chapter it lives under, and (where applicable) which NASA Space Place article fills the gap.

Used by: the daily DK + workbook routine in [`dk-workbook-routine.md`](dk-workbook-routine.md), particularly for the "chapter-matched reading" approach (Phase A).

## NASA integration — two distinct roles

### Role A: Astronomy gap-fill (Phase 6 chapters)
DK First Science Encyclopedia has limited astronomy coverage. For BFSU Phase 6 chapters (d1-d8), substitute the NASA Space Place article on the day's DK reading slot. Same 10-min/day routine, just NASA in place of DK.

### Role B: Monthly STEM activity (Phase A onwards, end of each phase)
NASA's [STEM @ Home K-4](https://www.nasa.gov/learning-resources/for-students-grades-k-4/) has packaged hands-on activities — one per month makes a "Friday celebration" at the end of each BFSU phase.

| Month | NASA activity | Pairs with phase end |
|---|---|---|
| June 2026 | Build a paper rocket | Phase 1 (Life) |
| July 2026 | Solar s'mores oven | Phase 2 (Energy) |
| August 2026 | Constellation viewer | (mid Phase 3) |
| September 2026 | Make a comet (with dry ice) | Phase 4 (Matter) |
| October 2026 | Cardboard Mars rover | Phase 6 (Earth/Space) — chapter-completion celebration |

### NASA hubs to bookmark

- [NASA Space Place](https://spaceplace.nasa.gov/) — kid-friendly articles + games + videos (PRIMARY)
- [NASA at Home for Kids and Families](https://www.nasa.gov/nasa-at-home-for-kids-and-families/) — activity index by grade
- [NASA Learning Resources hub](https://www.nasa.gov/learning-resources/) — full educator catalog
- [Learning Space with NASA at Home (JPL)](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/edu/resources/lesson-plan/learning-space-with-nasa-at-home/) — virtual hands-on lessons (the closest thing to "NASA Space Lab")
