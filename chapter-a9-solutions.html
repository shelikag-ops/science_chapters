🔭 Today's question

Where does the SUGAR GO when it dissolves?

Stir sugar in water. It vanishes! But the water tastes sweet. So... where IS the sugar?

🤔 Today's mission: crack the mystery of dissolving — and how solutions work!
🥤

🔬 What is a Solution?

💧 Solvent
The thing doing the dissolving (usually water).
🍬 Solute
The thing being dissolved (sugar, salt, etc.).
🥤 Solution
The mixture once dissolved (sweet water).
👁 Looks clear!
You can't see the bits — particles too small.

Tap each box!

🔍 What's Happening?

🍬Sugar bits
→
💧Water around them
→
➡️Sugar breaks into TINY particles
→
🫥Spread between water — invisible!
💡 Sugar didn't disappear — it broke into super tiny pieces!

🧂 Demo: Salt Test

Stir salt into water. Taste! Try to see it.

  • You can't see salt anymore.
  • But water tastes salty — proof it's there!
  • Now BOIL the water. Watch what's left after all water evaporates.
💡 Salt is left behind as a powder — it never disappeared!

🌡 What Helps Things DISSOLVE?

🔥 Heat
Hot water dissolves things faster (faster particles).
🌀 Stirring
Mixes solute around.
✂️ Smaller pieces
Powder dissolves faster than chunks.

Tap each box!

🕵️ MYSTERY #1

Why doesn't OIL mix with water?

Pour oil in water. They never blend — oil floats on top. Why?

Water particles love water particles. Oil particles love oil particles. They're too "different" to mix — like trying to mix marbles and beans!

🕵️ MYSTERY #2

Why does sugar dissolve faster in HOT tea?

Drop a sugar cube in hot tea — gone fast! In cold tea — slow.

Hot water particles move FASTER. They bump into sugar more, breaking it apart faster. Heat = speed!

🕵️ MYSTERY #3

Why is the OCEAN salty?

Rain is fresh. But oceans are salty. Where does the salt come from?

Rivers carry tiny bits of salt from rocks into oceans. Water EVAPORATES (leaving salt!), but salt stays. Over millions of years — VERY salty!

🍯 What Else Dissolves?

Try at home (with parent help):

  • 🍬 Sugar in water → YES (sweet!)
  • 🧂 Salt in water → YES (salty!)
  • 🛢 Oil in water → NO (separates)
  • 🪨 Sand in water → NO (sinks)
  • 🍋 Lemon juice in water → YES (acid!)

✨ What you now know

  • Dissolving = breaking into super-tiny particles in a liquid
  • Solute (gets dissolved) + solvent (does dissolving) = solution
  • Heat, stirring, smaller pieces all help dissolving
  • Some things don't dissolve (oil, sand)
  • Stuff dissolved is still there — boil water to prove it!
