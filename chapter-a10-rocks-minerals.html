🪨 The 3 Types of Rocks

🌋 Igneous
Cooled LAVA from volcanoes! (Granite, basalt)
🏝 Sedimentary
Layers of sand/dust pressed together! (Sandstone, limestone)
🔥 Metamorphic
Other rocks SQUISHED & HEATED into new rocks! (Marble, slate)

💎 Minerals: The Building Blocks

🪨 What are they?
Pure substances rocks are MADE of.
💎 Examples
Quartz, feldspar, mica, gold, salt!
🥚 Like ingredients
A rock is like cake — minerals are flour, sugar, etc.
⭐ Properties
Hardness, color, shine, shape — each is unique!

Tap each box!

✨ Crystals: Nature's Geometry

Crystals are minerals with a SPECIAL SHAPE.

  • Salt = cubes (look closely!)
  • Quartz = 6-sided pillars
  • Snowflakes = 6-pointed crystals
  • They form when liquids cool slowly OR water evaporates
💡 Atoms inside line up in PERFECT patterns!
🕵️ MYSTERY #1

How does a tiny SEED of a crystal grow huge?

Crystals can grow from tiny to giant. How?

Each new layer of atoms STACKS in the same pattern. Like building with LEGOs — same brick, over and over. As long as there's solute, they grow!

🕵️ MYSTERY #2

Why are mountain ROCKS often LAYERED?

Look at a cliffside — see the stripes? Why?

Sedimentary rocks form in LAYERS over millions of years. Each stripe = a different time period — like pages in Earth's history book!

🕵️ MYSTERY #3

Where does SOIL come from?

We learned in B-12: soil = rocks + dead stuff. But where do those rocks come from?

WEATHERING! Rain, wind, frost slowly break BIG rocks into small bits → eventually into soil. Wind + water = nature's blender, working over thousands of years!

🌍 Why Rocks Matter

🏠 Building
Houses, roads, monuments — all from rocks!
🍞 Food
Soil from weathered rocks feeds plants → us!
💎 Treasure
Gold, diamonds, gems are minerals!
⛽ Energy
Coal, oil come from old rocks!
