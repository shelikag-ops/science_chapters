## 2. The Big Question (slide #2)

> **"How did sailors cross OCEANS before GPS?"**

Hook with Vikings, Polynesians, Columbus. No tech, but they navigated. **HOW?**

This is a HOOK, but it's followed immediately by a CONCEPT slide — not by another mystery.

## 10. Reusable Pattern (for similar chapters)

This same structural approach should be used for:
- **D-7 Mass vs Weight** — Concept (gravity differs by mass) → Evidence → Application
- **D-8 Rocks & Fossils** — Concept (rock cycle geology) → Evidence (fossils) → Application
- Any other **single-concept** chapter where the topic has clear concept + clear evidence + clear applications

For **multi-concept merged chapters** (D-2 Time, D-3 Maps, D-4 Biomes), use 2 sessions and apply CEA twice (one per concept).
